About

A brief look at what I do and where I've done it.

I build ML and analytics solutions with a strong emphasis on clean data, measurable evaluation, and production readiness. My recent focus is on LLM applications — retrieval-augmented generation, agentic workflows, and natural-language interfaces built with LangChain, LangGraph, and HuggingFace.

At Lundbeck, a global pharmaceutical company, I architected time-series forecasting pipelines that improved accuracy by 22%, led an MLOps rollout on AWS SageMaker that cut model refresh time by 36%, and built data pipelines processing over 10 million records a day. Earlier, at PropTech startup Airriva, my pricing and surge-detection models drove a 30% increase in profits.

I enjoy simplifying complex systems — making outputs interpretable, pipelines maintainable, and results useful to both technical and non-technical stakeholders. Beyond work, I've led a 20+ engineer team at Omdena on computer-vision models for rooftop solar detection, and won 1st place in the UN "Unite Ideas" challenge for anti-corruption fraud detection technology.
